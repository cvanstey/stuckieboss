Foreacting The BRICS Nations Inflation Rate(Using Python)

The purpose of this project was to conduct a forecast on the BRICS Nations inflation rate over four years. The BRICS is made up of Brazil, Russia, India, China, and South Africa, and these are ranked among the world's fastest-growing emerging market economies, thanks to low labour costs, favourable demographics and abundant natural resources. The data used was sourced from the World Bank Data Bank last updated 15 September 2021. The forecasting model implemented was the AutoRegression Model.
